The Canerica Isles Guidebook

The Canerica Isles Guidebook is a project exploring my cultural identity through graphic design. The guidebook details life on the Canerica Isles, a fictional island nation that combines all the places I've ever lived in into one utopian society. In addition to the guidebook, I created a map to help the reader understand the climate and geography of the islands.
