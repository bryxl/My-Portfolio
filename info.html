About Me

Hi there!

I'm Brianne' De Los Santos, a Filipina-Canadian designer living in Florida, USA. I've spent the last four years honing my skills in design and I'm particularly interested in designing for screens in ways that are as inclusive and accessible as possible while still being fun and exciting.

About My Work: As a two-time immigrant, I frequently pay homages to the places I've lived in through my personal projects. When it comes to art and design, I often say that I like everything and anything. Different mediums, styles, artists, cultures – I can find something interesting about any topic. Through many experimentations I've found a particular passion for illustrating, animating, and front-end development.

At the moment, I'm balancing my part-time job as the graphic designer for the University of Florida Genetics Institute with my role as Co-Creative Director at TEDxUF. When I'm not working, you can catch me thrifting, watching video essays, or playing video games.
