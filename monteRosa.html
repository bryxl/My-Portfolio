Monte Rosa Hut
Motion Poster

A motion poster I made in school for the Monte Rosa Hut. I was inspired by vintage travel posters and postcards from the 50s and 60s.

"

Background

The Monte Rosa Hut is a mountain hut located in the Swiss Alps at an altitude of 2,883 metres. In terms of energy use, it is almost completely self-sufficient and will be used as a research station in the future to study how to use energy and resources efficiently.

Process

I wanted the Monte Rosa Hut to act like a lighthouse to a pair of weary travelers in the foreground. In order for the hut to appear warm and inviting, its surroundings had to appear cold and remote by comparison. I used a cool colour palette for the environment and a warmer yellow for the interior lights of the Monte Rosa.

On paper, I started with a thumbnail sketch of my composition before expanding to a more realized sketch. Then in Photoshop, I started to block in my shapes before rendering the final poster.
