I first fell in love with his music when my French teacher played his songs during class. Despite being unfamiliar with the language, I felt like I could understand what he was saying through his musicality and emotion alone. When I was examing the album, I felt a disconnect between the uptempo experimental music and the calm and restrained cover.

I created another version of the album cover to be displayed on different music streaming platforms. The physical redesigned album cover is reliant on its three-dimensional aspect and without the ability to display the back or the slip jacket I felt like a lot of the storytelling aspects would be lost. So instead, I chose to omit the storytelling entirely and just let the viewer focus on the impossible forms of the environment.

As for the banner, I simply used a close-up of the image I made with the impossible version of the Century Gothic typeface that Stromae commissioned from the original album designers.

Final Takeaways
