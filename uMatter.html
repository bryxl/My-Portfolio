U Matter, We Care
Student Ambassadors

A marketing campaign I tackled by myself to recruit ambassadors for the U Matter, We Care organization, and to promote its mission.

>

Background

U Matter, We Care Initiative aims to assist the community with: care related resources and programs focused on health, safety, and holistic well-being finding an appropriate network of support education and training access to tools for helping faculty, staff and family members help students pathways to get involved with the initiative.

Every year U Matter recruits more ambassadors with a different theme and a different slogan. The slogan for 2020 was "Small Acts, Great Impacts."
